ABOUT US

At NewFalls Dental, we want you to have an extraordinary dental experience.

We are an expert team of providers, devoted to your beautiful smiles. Our focus is to better your overall oral health in addition to addressing your immediate dental needs. With over 30 years of experience amongst our doctors and state-of-the-art equipment, NewFalls dental group is committed to delivering customized and painless treatments of the highest quality. Your comfort comes first for us.

Ours is a future-ready facility, with every nook and corner built with our patient in mind. With the innovative technology and comprehensive dental services that we offer, our dentists can take care of all your dental needs. We believe the experience our facility houses, speaks for itself. We understand how intimidating a dentist’s chair can be.
